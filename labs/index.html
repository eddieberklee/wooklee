Foraday

Foraday is a unique effort designed to help users organize and visualize their day better. This is an attempt to bring back the visual and spatial cues provided by an analog clock, largely lost in our shift to a digital experience.

The app features an intuitive clock design that seamlessly fuses the AM and PM. This design allows users to see an event that starts in the morning and lasts into the afternoon, without having to use two separate clocks.
